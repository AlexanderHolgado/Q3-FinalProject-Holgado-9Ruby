Jimmy Donaldson, Mr. Beast

Jimmy Donaldson, better known as MrBeast, is an American internet celebrity who is known for his generous acts of kindness, his faced paced videos, and some of his companies like MrBeast Burger and Feastables.

Jimmy was boen in May 7, 1998, grew up in Greenville, North Caronlina. And by the age of 13, he made Youtube videos under the name "MrBeast6000". His early content ranged from Let's Plays to "videos estimating the wealth of other YouTubers". His channel became viral in 2017 when MrBeast made a video called "counting to 100,000". Since then, MrBeast's videos have grown more and more when it comes to popularity. Now he owns multiple channels such as "MrBeast", "MrBeast Gaming", and "Beast Philanthropy"

Jimmy Donaldson is also known for his donations and fundraisers which he co-created with Mark Rober such as "Team Trees" and "Team Sea"s. Because of his success, he has appeared in places such as the Kids Choice Awards, and has appeared in movies such as "Teenage Mutant Nigja Turtles: Mutant Mayhem"
